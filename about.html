Data Pre-Processing

This section outlines all steps necessary to prepare the data before the final implementation.

  • Data Acqusition

    We experimented with the Covid-19 All Vaccine Tweets dataset. The dataset is hosted on an open-source platform Kaggle. It is licensed under CC0: Public Domain. It used relevant search terms using the ‘Tweepy’ python module from Twitter API. It had started collecting regarding the relevant hashtags from over the world in February 2021 and has been updated daily since.

  • Data Cleaning

    The dataset provides information in a tabular format divided by numerous attributes. Some of the given data types like the date and time of the post provide no particular use while extracting the sentiment.

    So, during the cleaning of the data set, we remove the extraneous columns, duplicate tweets, stop-words, special characters to lessen the noisy data present. This also assures the exclusion of any #hashtags, URL(s), user-handles, punctuation, whitespaces, etc.

  • Tokenisation

    For this operation, the textual corpus is broken into meaningful subparts called 'tokens'. These are necessary input elements for further lexical and semantic exploration of the corpus. The creation of n-gram tokens gives meaning to the word itself and whether it is annotated as positive or negative perception

  • Lemmatization

    This is necessary as writers use a variety of tenses and formats when formulating their posts. Also, many words have their related derivatives. To reduce the variation of tones and inflection, we transfigure the text to its base form. To remove derivational affixes and use the correct morphological format of the word, stemming references a vocabulary and removes inflectional endings.

  • Feature Extraction

    This module provides word optimization, vectorization, and embedding features. We use it to map the words to their significant numbered weights, which greatly help in processing the data into neural networks. It illustrates the semantic relation of sentence composition. This is essential for eliciting appropriate erudition and the NLU.

  • Assigning Polarity

    To analyze the predictions of our implementation models, we have labeled the sentiments to all the tweets using TextBlob.
