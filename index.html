About

Sentiment analysis refers to categorizing the emotions articulated in the extracted data. In this project, Neural Network models (CNN, RNN-LSTM) are used to predict the sentiment of Covid-19 Vaccination Tweets.

Moreover, we have also introduced Federated Learning, a distributed, on-device learning framework for sentiment analysis. Federated Learning is a distributed ML approach that facilitates training on an extensive canon of decentralized text rooming on gadgets like smartphones.
